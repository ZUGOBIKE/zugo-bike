# References

## Brand & Design Inspiration

ZUGO is a premium lifestyle brand. Clean, modern design with strong product storytelling. Think minimal and high-trust, not flashy.

- **VanMoof** — clean, modern e-bike brand aesthetic
- **Super73** — lifestyle-focused electric bike brand energy
- **Tesla** — minimal, product-first experience
- **Apple** — simple, premium, high-trust design language
- **ZUGO Bike** — https://www.zugobike.com

## Content & SEO Style

Educational, high-value content that builds trust and authority before selling.

- **REI** — excellent educational guides and product content
- **HubSpot** — clear, structured, SEO-driven content strategy

## Functionality & Systems

Simple, intuitive systems that scale and are easy to maintain.

- **Notion** — clean structure and organization
- **Stripe** — clear, efficient, developer-friendly systems

## ZUGO Typography

- **Primary typeface:** Helvetica Neue
- **Headline weight:** 93 Black Extended
- **Body weight:** 43 Light Extended
- **Web fallback:** `'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`
- **Alignment:** left-aligned, rag right — never centered for multi-line text
- **Line length:** 45–70 characters

## Brand Voice & Feel

- **Archetype:** The Creator — innovative, inspirational, daring, imaginative
- Premium, fun, and powerful
- Educate and build trust — not just sell
- Inspire exploration and adventure
- Heritage aesthetics with modern innovation
- Documentary-style photography: cool, high contrast, dynamic
- The website should guide users clearly toward purchase
- Systems should reduce manual work and support business scaling

## Target Audience

Determined, active people who value quality outdoor equipment and heritage aesthetics. Riders who see e-bikes as a lifestyle — not just transportation. Adventure-seekers who want performance across all terrains.
